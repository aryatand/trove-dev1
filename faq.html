How can I make sure I keep up these healthy and organized habits throughout college?
Hold yourself accountable! You can even do check-ins with yourself at the end of each week and rate how organized you were the past week. This will make you more aware of how you are living.


How can I prioritize tasks within a large project so I don't procrastinate?
Physically writing down each step until the project is done before you even start is a great way to visualize everything you have to do. This also makes it much more manageable to tackle each small task rather than feeling intimidated by the whole project. Take it step by step!


I keep getting distracted when I am studying, how do I fix this?
Personally, I (Arya) use the Pomodoro method when I really need to do work yet, I sometimes just cannot put my phone down. This forces me to work for a short amount of time and take a break. Time also passes by super fast using this method. Sometimes this doesn't work for everyone, so maybe divide up your time by each task on your agenda. Do one task then spend 5 minutes taking a break and go through your to-do list like that!
